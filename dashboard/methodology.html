Methodology

How every figure is sourced, computed, and lensed, and why this is a collateral-risk terminal, not a registry. ● Aave Horizon + Morpho
● What this is (and isn't)

The question. Which tokenized real-world assets are deployed in DeFi lending, and how do they behave as collateral? This is not a registry of what RWA exists, nor a measure of protocol TVL. It's the risk and usage of RWA as collateral: per-position (asset × venue) risk (LTV, liquidation threshold, NAV freshness / grade) and usage (holders, flows), across venues.

Scope. Spans Aave Horizon (on-chain) and Morpho (GraphQL). Coverage is the assets actually listed/used on those venues, not the whole tokenized-RWA market (which is multiples larger). "RWA AUM" here = the AUM of the assets that appear on a venue.

The two numbers that are easy to confuse

  • Asset AUM, the size of the fund (issuer-reported or supply×NAV). This is the market-size number and the headline.
  • Venue-supplied, how much of that asset is deposited into Horizon. A venue position, not fund size. USYC has $2.85B AUM but $0 on Horizon, both true, different lenses.

Every figure travels with its lens (asset-level vs venue; stablecoins in/out; NAV vs price; as-of block).

● Where the data comes from

On-chain, your Alchemy RPC (batched via Multicall3)

  • Reserve set, LTV, liquidation threshold, supplied, supply APY → AaveProtocolDataProvider (getAllReservesTokens, getReserveData). Enumerated dynamically, never hardcoded.
  • Price + NAV → AaveOracle (getAssetPrice, getSourceOfAsset) → the LlamaGuard / Chainlink / RedStone feeds (latestRoundData / latestAnswer).
  • Each token's totalSupply (ERC-20).

Off-chain issuer APIs (timestamped NAV)

  • Superstate, USTB / USCC NAV, AUM, as-of date.
  • Hashnote, USYC price, supply, timestamp.
  • Centrifuge GraphQL (api.centrifuge.io), JTRSY / JAAA NAV (tokenPrice) + freshness (tokenPriceComputedAt). Supply still on-chain (their totalIssuance aggregate is broken).

Storage & cadence

A Cloudflare cron worker reads the above every 5 minutes and appends a snapshot to Neon (Postgres), the history that future time-series charts draw from.

Tier-3 reconciliation (wired)

DefiLlama (api.llama.fi/protocol/aave-horizon-rwa), independent Horizon TVL, cross-checked against our on-chain number and shown as a Verified / Degraded badge (±5% tolerance).

Planned

AaveKit GraphQL (Aave-side cross-check); Envio (event history, flows, holders).

● How each metric is computed
  • Asset AUM = issuer-reported AUM where an API exists (Superstate, Hashnote), else tokenTotalSupply × NAV.
  • NAV = on-chain oracle (latestRoundData.answer), with the issuer API as the value + freshness source for value-only feeds.
  • NAV freshness = age of the on-chain updatedAt, or the issuer API's timestamp. Three states: fresh, stale (>48h), value-only (a price reads but the on-chain adapter exposes no timestamp, e.g. the LlamaGuard "USD Scaled" feeds; freshness must come from the issuer).
  • Venue-supplied = Horizon aToken balance × oracle price. Kept distinct from AUM.
  • Composition = Σ asset AUM grouped by asset class / issuer.
  • Data-quality grade (A–D) = deductions for unmapped assets, stale/unreadable NAV (major) and value-only feeds (minor).
● Data-quality rules (the lint)
  • Dynamic enumeration. The reserve set comes from getReservesList() live, never a static list. (This is how we caught ACRED, a reserve the spec didn't list.)
  • Identity by address. Joins are on the contract address, never the ticker.
  • Anti-double-count. Market size is asset-level AUM, not Σ(supply×price) across venues; venue-supplied is reported separately.
  • NAV staleness / value-only. Freshness is flagged, never shown as fresh when it isn't.
  • Stablecoins split out. Included but shown as a separate component, at full circulating supply.
● Known limitations
  • Horizon-only. Not the full RWA market, Phase 2 adds Morpho and direct issuer tokens.
  • Stablecoins = full supply. The $53B stablecoin figure is total circulating supply (USDC-dominated), not a Horizon number.
  • No Distributed/Represented yet. No authoritative public source wired.
  • No value-weighted yield yet. Needs issuer /yield endpoints.
  • JTRSY / JAAA freshness. Centrifuge publishes no timestamped feed and the on-chain adapter is value-only, so their NAV age is honestly "off-chain," not missing.
